Fighter Equipment Guide

Equipment Basics

Fighter equipment is crucial for maximizing your combat effectiveness. Each fighter can equip up to 4 pieces of equipment, and proper selection can dramatically improve performance.

Equipment Types

  • Weapons - Increase attack power and critical chance
  • Armor - Boost defense and HP for survivability
  • Accessories - Provide specialized bonuses like speed or skill damage
  • Special Items - Unique equipment with powerful effects

Rarity Tiers

  • Rare (Green) - Basic equipment, max level 20
  • Epic (Purple) - Advanced equipment, max level 25
  • Legendary (Orange) - Premium equipment, max level 30

Upgrade Strategy

Focus on upgrading equipment in this order:

  1. Main weapon for damage output
  2. Primary armor for survivability
  3. Accessories for specialized bonuses
  4. Secondary items for completion bonuses

Material Management

Equipment materials are precious resources. Prioritize:

  • Legendary fighters get first priority for materials
  • Focus on main combat fighters over support roles
  • Save rare materials for high-level upgrades (25+)
  • Use events to farm materials efficiently

Fighter Experience Guide

XP Sources

Fighters gain experience from multiple sources. Understanding the most efficient methods saves time and resources.

Primary XP Sources

  • Combat - Earn XP from PvP battles and monster hunts
  • XP Items - Use fighter XP books and potions
  • Events - Participate in fighter training events
  • Daily Missions - Complete fighter-specific tasks

Leveling Efficiency

XP requirements scale exponentially. Focus efforts wisely:

  • Levels 1-20: Use low-level XP items freely
  • Levels 20-35: Save high-value XP items for this range
  • Levels 35-45: Use premium XP items and focus on main fighters
  • Levels 45-50: Reserve legendary XP items for final push

XP Multipliers

Several bonuses can increase XP gain:

  • VIP bonuses provide flat XP increases
  • Guild research can boost fighter XP gain
  • Special events offer 2x or 3x XP periods
  • Fighter training facilities provide passive XP

Strategic Priorities

Don't spread XP too thin across all fighters:

  1. Max your main combat fighter first
  2. Level support fighters to key breakpoints
  3. Focus on fighters you use in active combat
  4. Keep backup fighters at moderate levels

Fighter Medals Guide

Medal System Overview

Fighter medals are used to upgrade fighter skills, making them essential for competitive play.

Medal Requirements by Rarity

  • Rare Fighters - 325 medals for max skills (5/5/5/5)
  • Epic Fighters - 450 medals for max skills (5/5/5/5)
  • Legendary Fighters - 690 medals for max skills (5/5/5/5)

Leap Medal Requirements

Additional medals needed for leaping (advancing rarity):

  • Rare to Epic: 800 leap medals
  • Epic to Legendary: 1,180 leap medals
  • Legendary enhancement: 700 medals

Medal Sources

  • Events - Fighter-focused events offer the best medal rates
  • Shop Purchases - Use gems to buy medal packages
  • Daily Missions - Complete fighter tasks for medals
  • Guild Activities - Guild wars and hunts provide medals

Investment Strategy

Medals are scarce, so invest strategically:

  1. Focus on one legendary fighter at a time
  2. Upgrade skill 1 first (highest impact)
  3. Balance skills 2-4 based on fighter role
  4. Save leap medals for fighters you'll use long-term

Fighter Statue Stars Guide

Statue System

Fighter statues provide permanent bonuses to all fighters of that type. This is a late-game progression system that provides significant power increases.

How Statue Stars Work

  • Each fighter type has its own statue
  • Stars are earned by sacrificing duplicate fighters
  • Each star provides increasing bonuses
  • Bonuses apply to ALL fighters of that type

Star Requirements

Stars become increasingly expensive to unlock:

  • 1-3 Stars: Use common/rare duplicates freely
  • 4-6 Stars: Requires epic fighter duplicates
  • 7-10 Stars: Demands legendary fighter sacrifices
  • 11+ Stars: Extremely expensive, only for endgame

Priority Order

Focus statue upgrades on fighters you use most:

  1. Main DPS fighters (usually Hitters)
  2. Tank fighters for survivability
  3. Support fighters for utility
  4. Specialized fighters based on your strategy

Statue Bonuses

  • Attack power increases
  • Defense and HP bonuses
  • Skill effectiveness improvements
  • Special abilities at higher star levels

Long-term Strategy

Statue progression is a marathon, not a sprint:

  • Save duplicate legendary fighters for statues
  • Don't sacrifice fighters you might need later
  • Plan statue upgrades around major events
  • Consider the global bonuses vs individual fighter power

Mech Force Guide

Force System Overview

Mech Force represents the raw power and level of your mech. Higher force levels provide increased combat effectiveness and unlock new capabilities.

Force Leveling

  • Max Level - Both Epic and Legendary mechs cap at Force Level 60
  • Requirements - Uses Chips and Cores for upgrades
  • Scaling - Costs increase exponentially at higher levels

Resource Management

Force upgrades require careful resource planning:

  • Chips are the primary currency for force upgrades
  • Cores provide additional power boosts
  • Higher levels require thousands of each resource
  • Focus upgrades on your main battle mech first

Upgrade Strategy

  1. Rush to Level 30 for core functionality
  2. Level 40 provides significant power spike
  3. Level 50+ requires major resource investment
  4. Level 60 is endgame goal for main mech

Force Benefits

  • Increased base damage and defense
  • Higher HP and survivability
  • Access to advanced mech abilities
  • Better performance in large battles

Mech Skills Guide

Skill System

Mech skills provide specialized abilities and bonuses. Each mech has multiple skill slots that can be upgraded independently.

Skill Types

  • Active Skills - Triggered abilities with cooldowns
  • Passive Skills - Permanent bonuses to combat
  • Utility Skills - Non-combat benefits like movement speed
  • Ultimate Skills - Powerful abilities unlocked at high levels

Skill Leveling

  • Max Level: 30 for all skills
  • Resource: Skill Manuals required for upgrades
  • Each skill type has different upgrade costs
  • Some skills have breakpoints at levels 10, 20, 30

Upgrade Priority

Focus skill upgrades based on your mech's role:

  1. DPS Mechs - Prioritize damage-dealing active skills
  2. Tank Mechs - Focus on defensive and HP skills
  3. Support Mechs - Upgrade utility and team-buff skills
  4. Hybrid Mechs - Balance between primary and secondary roles

Skill Combinations

Some skills work better together:

  • Damage skills pair well with critical chance boosts
  • Defensive skills complement HP and healing abilities
  • Speed skills enhance both combat and utility
  • Area effect skills benefit from range increases

Mech Operators Guide

Operator System

Operators are the pilots who control mechs. Each operator brings unique bonuses and can be trained to improve their effectiveness.

Operator Types

  • Primary Operator - Main pilot with base stats
  • Co-Pilot - Secondary operator for additional bonuses
  • Specialist - Operators with unique abilities

Operator Training

Operators can be improved through various methods:

  • Experience points from combat and training
  • Skill points allocated to different abilities
  • Equipment specific to operators
  • Specialized training programs

Synergy Bonuses

Certain operator combinations provide bonuses:

  • Same faction operators work better together
  • Complementary skills provide team bonuses
  • High-level operators unlock special abilities
  • Rare operators provide unique effects

Operator Strategy

  1. Match operator specialties to mech roles
  2. Train primary operators first for immediate benefits
  3. Look for synergy opportunities with multiple operators
  4. Save rare operators for main battle mechs

Troop Unit Skills Guide

Unit Skill System

Each troop type has unique skills that can be upgraded to improve combat effectiveness. Understanding these skills is crucial for army optimization.

Skill Categories

  • Combat Skills - Direct damage and defensive abilities
  • Formation Skills - Bonuses when troops work together
  • Tactical Skills - Strategic abilities like flanking or ambush
  • Support Skills - Healing, buffs, and utility effects

Skill Upgrade Materials

Different materials are needed for skill upgrades:

  • Skill Books - Basic upgrade material for all units
  • Advanced Manuals - Required for higher skill levels
  • Specialized Tomes - Unique to specific troop types
  • Master Scrolls - Rare materials for maximum levels

Upgrade Priority

  1. Focus on your main army composition first
  2. Upgrade damage skills before defensive ones
  3. Prioritize skills that affect multiple unit types
  4. Save rare materials for tier 5-6 troops

Troop Weapons Guide

Weapon Systems

Troops can be equipped with various weapons that significantly impact their combat performance.

Weapon Types

  • Melee Weapons - Swords, axes, and close-combat gear
  • Ranged Weapons - Bows, guns, and projectile weapons
  • Heavy Weapons - Artillery, siege equipment, explosives
  • Special Weapons - Unique or magical armaments

Weapon Upgrades

Weapons can be enhanced through several methods:

  • Level upgrades using weapon materials
  • Quality improvements (Common → Rare → Epic)
  • Enchantments for special effects
  • Weapon mastery through combat experience

Tactical Considerations

  • Match weapons to troop strengths and roles
  • Consider enemy composition when choosing weapons
  • Balance between damage and utility effects
  • Account for weapon maintenance and durability

Troop Gear Guide

Beyond weapons, troops can equip various gear pieces that provide defensive bonuses, utility effects, and specialized capabilities.

Gear Categories

  • Armor Sets - Helmets, chest pieces, boots for defense
  • Accessories - Rings, amulets, badges for special bonuses
  • Utility Gear - Medkits, tools, communication equipment

Gear Bonuses

Different gear provides various benefits:

  • Defense increases for better survivability
  • Speed bonuses for faster movement and deployment

  • Combat skill enhancements and special abilities

Set Bonuses

Equipping complete gear sets provides additional bonuses:

  • 2-piece sets: Basic stat improvements
  • 4-piece sets: Moderate skill bonuses

Gear Strategy

  4. Balance between offensive and defensive gear based on strategy

Kingdom vs Kingdom (KVK) Guide

KVK Overview

Kingdom vs Kingdom is the premier competitive event in Age of Apes, where entire kingdoms battle for supremacy and valuable rewards.

Pre-KVK Preparation

  • Resource Stockpiling - Save speedups, healing items, and troops
  • Alliance Coordination - Plan strategies with alliance members
  • Fighter Preparation - Ensure main fighters are maxed
  • Equipment Check - Optimize all equipment loadouts

KVK Phases

  1. Preparation Phase - 3 days to prepare and strategize
  2. Initial Battles - First 2 days of combat
  3. Main Event - Peak competition period (3-4 days)
  4. Final Push - Last day decisive battles

Scoring System

Points are earned through various activities:

  • Enemy troop eliminations (major points)
  • Building destruction and occupation
  • Resource gathering in enemy territory
  • Completing special KVK objectives

Strategic Tips

  • Focus on coordinated attacks rather than solo raids
  • Protect gatherers and builders from enemy attacks
  • Use teleports strategically to avoid losses
  • Save strongest troops for decisive battles

Interstellar Clash Guide

Event Overview

Interstellar Clash is a cross-server competitive event where the strongest players from different kingdoms compete for ultimate prizes.

Qualification Process

  • Kingdom Rankings - Top players from each kingdom qualify
  • Power Requirements - Minimum power threshold must be met
  • Activity Level - Recent activity and participation required
  • Alliance Standing - Good standing in home alliance

Event Structure

  1. Registration - Sign up during qualification period
  2. Group Stage - Initial battles to determine seeding
  3. Bracket Rounds - Tournament-style elimination
  4. Finals - Championship matches for top prizes

Competition Format

Interstellar Clash features multiple competition types:

  • Individual combat tournaments
  • Team-based alliance battles
  • Resource control challenges
  • Special objective missions

Preparation Strategy

  • Max out all fighters and equipment before the event
  • Study opponent strategies from previous clashes
  • Coordinate with alliance for team events
  • Prepare multiple army compositions for different scenarios

Rewards

Interstellar Clash offers the best rewards in the game:

  • Exclusive legendary fighters and equipment
  • Massive resource packages and speedups
  • Special titles and cosmetic items
  • Premium currency and rare materials

Gacha Events Guide

Gacha System

Gacha events offer chances to obtain rare fighters, equipment, and resources through luck-based draws.

Event Types

  • Fighter Gacha - New legendary fighters and upgrades
  • Equipment Gacha - Rare weapons and armor sets
  • Resource Gacha - Speedups, materials, and currency
  • Limited Events - Special themed gacha with exclusive items

Pull Mechanics

Understanding rates and pity systems:

  • Base rates for different rarity tiers
  • Pity counters that guarantee high-rarity pulls
  • Rate-up events with increased chances
  • Multi-pull bonuses and discounts

Strategic Spending

  1. Save for Rate-Ups - Wait for increased chances on desired items
  2. Plan for Pity - Ensure you have enough pulls to reach guaranteed rewards
  3. Focus Targets - Don't spread pulls across too many different events
  4. Value Assessment - Compare event value to direct purchase options

Budget Management

Gacha events can be expensive, so plan carefully:

  • Set monthly spending limits and stick to them
  • Prioritize events with items you actually need
  • Take advantage of first-time purchase bonuses
  • Consider long-term value vs immediate gratification

F2P Strategy

Free-to-play players can still benefit from gacha events:

  • Use free daily pulls when available
  • Save premium currency for guaranteed reward events
  • Focus on events that offer useful resources over rare fighters
  • Take advantage of event login bonuses and missions

Server Transfer Guide

When to Transfer

Server transfers should be considered carefully:

  • Your current server becomes too competitive
  • Looking for more active alliances
  • Following friends to a new server
  • Starting fresh in a newer server environment

Transfer Requirements

  • Account must be below certain power level
  • No recent combat activity
  • Transfer tokens required (purchased or earned)
  • Destination server must have available slots

What Transfers

Most progress carries over:

  • All fighters, equipment, and upgrades
  • Research progress and building levels
  • VIP level and purchased items
  • Most inventory items and resources

What Doesn't Transfer

  • Alliance membership and positions
  • Server-specific rankings and achievements
  • Some event progress and rewards
  • Mail and friend lists

Advanced Tips

Resource Management

  • Shielding - Use peace shields strategically during offline periods
  • Banking - Keep resources below protection limits when possible
  • Timing - Upgrade during events for maximum efficiency
  • Prioritization - Focus on power-generating upgrades first

Alliance Strategy

  • Participate in all alliance activities for rewards
  • Coordinate with members for major objectives
  • Share resources and help with construction
  • Maintain good relations for long-term benefits

Combat Optimization

  • Study enemy compositions and adapt accordingly
  • Use terrain advantages when possible
  • Time attacks for maximum impact
  • Always have healing items ready

Long-term Planning

  • Set monthly goals for progression
  • Plan major upgrades around events
  • Build relationships with other players
  • Stay informed about game updates and meta changes

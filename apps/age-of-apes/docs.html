Game Data Files

JSON data files available for integration:

  • data/buildings.json — Building upgrade costs and times (City Hall, Research Lab, Warehouse)
  • data/fighters.json — Fighter XP tables (Rare/Epic/Legendary), medals, and fighter types
  • data/troops.json — Troop training costs and power per tier (T1-T6)
  • data/research.json — Research trees (Civil, Military, Electronic, Weapons)
  • data/mechs.json — Mech upgrade costs (chips, cores, manuals, power)
  • data/equipment.json — Equipment material requirements by rarity and level

# Sri Lanka — Luxury Travel Research Summary

*Last updated: February 2026*

## Overview

Sri Lanka is a teardrop-shaped island offering pristine beaches, misty hill country, ancient ruins, world-class wildlife, and warm hospitality. It has rebounded strongly as a luxury destination post-2022.

**Best time to visit:** December–April (south/west coast); April–September (east coast)

## Key Regions

1. **Colombo** — Gateway city, 1-2 nights, fine dining & culture
2. **Galle & South Coast** — Highest luxury hotel concentration, beaches, whale watching
3. **Hill Country** — Tea plantations, scenic trains, cooler climate
4. **Cultural Triangle** — UNESCO sites, Sigiriya Rock, elephants
5. **Yala & Wildlife** — World's best leopard safaris
6. **East Coast** — Emerging luxury, surfing, diving, uncrowded beaches
7. **West Coast** — Bawa heritage, easy airport access

- 🐆 Leopard safari at Yala (Feb–Jul best)

## Practical Notes

- **Getting around:** Private car + driver-guide is standard (~$50-80/day). Domestic flights and helicopter transfers available.
- **Visa:** Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) required, available online
- **Safety:** Generally safe. Post-2022 economic crisis stabilized.
- **Currency:** Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR)
- **Health:** No mandatory vaccinations. Drink bottled water.

Data Sources

Questions Bank: data/questions.json — 120 questions across 5 categories (slang, events, pop culture, lifestyle, technology) covering 6 generations.

Scoring: Each question has hidden generation weights. Correct answers award full points; incorrect answers award 20% partial credit. Final result shows percentage breakdown across all generations.

Generations Covered

Silent Generation (1928–1945) — WWII, Big Band, rotary phones
Baby Boomers (1946–1964) — Vietnam, Woodstock, Apollo, disco
Gen X (1965–1980) — MTV, grunge, Berlin Wall, latchkey kids
Millennials (1981–1996) — 9/11, Y2K, MySpace, iPhone
Gen Z (1997–2012) — TikTok, COVID, climate activism, streaming
Gen Alpha (2013+) — iPad kids, AI, Roblox, Skibidi

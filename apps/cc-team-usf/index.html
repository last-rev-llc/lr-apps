Join Our Team

Since 1855, The University of San Francisco has been a longstanding member of the San Francisco community committed to service, education, and social causes. The driving focus of our university is to "Change the World from Here" — we are committed to making the world a better place, more fair, more livable, and more just for all people.


Team USF is the exclusive corporate partner program of USF Athletics and the University of San Francisco. Team USF provides corporate brands with unique positioning within USF Athletics and the University. Team USF partners align their corporate brand with the brand, values, and traditions of USF Athletics, and reach a regional and national audience in a positive and impactful manner.

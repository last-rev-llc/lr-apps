Dominique Debay is a luxury travel brand that curates extraordinary experiences in destinations like Bali, the Maldives, and Morocco. Their team was spending hours researching properties, vetting experiences, and compiling itinerary options for high-net-worth clients. We used AlphaClaw to build them a dedicated research platform that dramatically accelerates this process.

The Research Bottleneck

Luxury travel isn't about booking hotels on Expedia. It's about knowing that a particular villa in Ubud has a private chef who studied under a Michelin-starred mentor, or that a specific riad in Marrakech has a rooftop terrace with Atlas Mountain views that's only available for exclusive buyouts. This knowledge lives in the heads of experienced travel designers — and in dozens of browser bookmarks, PDFs, and email threads.

When a new client inquiry came in, the research phase could take 6-8 hours per destination. The team needed a way to centralize their institutional knowledge and accelerate the research-to-proposal pipeline.

What AlphaClaw Built

AlphaClaw created a research platform with a curated property database, experience catalog, and itinerary builder. The system allows the Dominique Debay team to search and filter properties by destination, price tier, style (romantic, adventure, wellness, family), and specific amenities. Each property has detailed cards with insider notes, photos, and pricing ranges.

The itinerary builder lets designers drag properties and experiences into a day-by-day timeline, automatically calculating travel times between locations and flagging logistical conflicts. The AI also suggests complementary experiences based on the client's stated preferences.

Key Features

  • Property database — searchable catalog of vetted luxury properties with insider notes, seasonal availability, and pricing tiers
  • Experience catalog — curated activities from private cooking classes to helicopter tours, tagged by destination and interest category
  • AI-powered matching — input a client brief and get recommended properties and experiences ranked by fit
  • Itinerary builder — visual day-by-day planner with drag-and-drop, travel time calculations, and conflict detection
  • Proposal export — one-click generation of beautiful, branded PDF proposals from the itinerary
  • Seasonal intelligence — alerts about weather patterns, peak seasons, and local events that might affect the experience

The Build

AlphaClaw built the platform over a series of sessions totaling about four hours. The property database schema, search interface, and itinerary builder were created in the first session. The AI matching engine and proposal export were added in follow-up iterations based on feedback from the Dominique Debay team.

The design uses warm, luxurious tones — cream backgrounds, gold accents, elegant typography — matching the Dominique Debay brand aesthetic. AlphaClaw nailed the visual tone from a simple description of "luxury, warm, editorial."

Impact

Research time per destination dropped from 6-8 hours to under 2 hours. The proposal quality improved because the itinerary builder catches logistical issues that designers might miss. Client response rates to proposals increased by 25% thanks to the polished PDF exports.

Key Features

What It Does

Jira Integration

Pull stories and epics directly from Jira. Two-way sync keeps everything in alignment — update here or there, it stays consistent.

Velocity Tracking

Automatic velocity calculation across sprints. See trends, predict capacity, and identify when the team is overcommitted.

Capacity Planning

Factor in PTO, holidays, and individual availability. AI recommends optimal sprint loads based on historical performance.

Sprint Goals

Define clear sprint goals and track progress in real-time. Daily burndown charts and blocker alerts keep the team focused.

How it works

From spark to shipped.

Four steps. No complexity. Ideas flow through a system built to surface the best ones and let the rest fade.

Capture

Ideas arrive from anywhere — Slack messages, AI brainstorm sessions, team meetings, or quick manual entry. Everything gets a card.

Score

AI evaluates feasibility (1–10) and impact (1–10) to generate a composite score. Add your own effort estimate and star rating.

Prioritize

Filter by category, sort by composite score, hide what doesn't matter. The highest-impact ideas rise to the top automatically.

Ship

Move the best ideas to in-progress. Track completion. Archive what's done. Your backlog stays clean and current.
Scoring

Data beats opinions.

Every idea gets a composite score weighted toward impact. The best ideas surface themselves.
// composite score
score = (feasibility × 0.4)
      + (impact × 0.6)

// example
8 × 0.4 + 9 × 0.6 = 8.6

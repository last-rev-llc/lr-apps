📊 How We Calculate Your Savings

Our projections are grounded in peer-reviewed research and industry data from leading consulting firms. McKinsey Global Institute found that generative AI and related technologies can automate work activities that absorb 60–70% of employees' time, up from 50% with traditional automation alone. Controlled productivity studies show that employees using AI tools experience an average 40% productivity boost, with gains ranging from 25–55% depending on the function (Stanford/MIT, 2023). Deloitte's 2024 State of AI Report found that organizations deploying AI at scale report an average of $3.70 returned for every $1 invested in AI initiatives. Gartner predicts that by 2026, 20% of organizations will use AI to eliminate more than half of current middle management positions, redirecting those resources to higher-value work.

This calculator uses conservative estimates: we assume AI can reduce 30% of meeting time (through automated summaries, scheduling, and prep) and 40% of manual/repetitive task time (through workflow automation, data entry, reporting, and document generation). These rates sit at the lower end of documented ranges to provide realistic, achievable projections for your organization.

Sources: McKinsey (2023) · Stanford/MIT Study (2023) · Deloitte (2024) · Gartner (2024)
